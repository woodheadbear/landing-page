Welcome to Brave Dogs Inc.

Across cinematic deserts and through the frozen wilds of imagination, stand the true, four-legged paladins: the brave dogs, whose loyalty is the very bedrock of our salvation. With a bark that shatters evil and paws that tread where human fear retreats, these glorious canine heroes routinely defy the impossible, whether piloting starships through alien fleets, or simply nudging a heartbroken child back from the brink of despair. Their coats shine with an unassuming pomp, for they require no medals, demanding only a loving scratch behind the ears as they silently accept their destiny as the planet's ultimate, tail-wagging security detail.
